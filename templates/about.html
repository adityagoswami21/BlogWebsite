About Me

This is what I do.

Hi there! I'm Aditya Goswami, a passionate tech enthusiast, Python developer, and electrical engineering student with a knack for creating innovative solutions. My journey into the world of engineering and technology has been an exciting blend of learning, experimentation, and personal growth. As I pursue my BTech in Electrical Engineering, I’ve developed a strong foundation in coding, problem-solving, and applying technical knowledge to real-world scenarios.

When I'm not coding or experimenting with dielectric materials, you can find me exploring creative avenues. I was the co-founder of Blissful Moments Candles, where I discovered the art of blending passion with entrepreneurship.

My love for technology extends to programming, where I thrive on building creative and functional projects. Beyond coding, I’m a creative soul who finds inspiration in sketching. For me, sketching is more than just a hobby—it’s a way to express emotions and capture the beauty of the world around me. With every pencil stroke, I aim to bring ideas to life on paper, whether it’s a realistic portrait or an imaginative design.

When I’m not immersed in tech or art, I’m out on my motorcycle, chasing the thrill of the open road. Riding offers me a sense of freedom and adventure, allowing me to explore new places and connect with the world in a unique way. Additionally, I enjoy playing basketball, where I not only get to stay active but also embrace the teamwork and excitement of the game.

This blog is a reflection of my passions, experiences, and aspirations. It’s a space where I share my journey, dive into the intersection of creativity and technology, and connect with like-minded individuals. Whether you’re here for insights, inspiration, or just a good read, I’m thrilled to have you along for the ride.
